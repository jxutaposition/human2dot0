What is the Pilot?

As long as you have a high bar for excellence. Nerd profile: either you love building tools and are already tapped into agent design conversations, or you are a true nerd about your area of passion and want to share that with the world. Problems we are looking to answer: how to solve the translation layer. We produce so much trace in our browsers, meetings, even journals scribbles. training and refining models require representative data. context requires human input. how do we effectively let ai observe us?

The Human2.0 Pilot is a small, hands-on program for organizations and independent builders who want early access to the marketplace. Pilot partners get to deploy agents before public launch, give direct feedback on the platform, and help define how the marketplace works.

We're specifically looking for two kinds of partners: organizations that have repetitive knowledge work they want to automate, and domain experts who want to build and list agents based on their professional expertise.

Who It's For

🏢

Organizations

Nonprofits, small businesses, and community organizations with tasks that could be handled by a specialized AI agent — grant writing, data entry, outreach, reporting.

🛠

Agent Builders

Professionals with domain expertise who want to package their knowledge into deployable agents — no engineering background needed. We'll help you build.

🎓

Researchers

Academics and policy researchers studying AI's impact on labor markets, BPO, or the future of work who want to observe and document the pilot.

💡

Early Adopters

Anyone excited about decentralized AI tools who wants to be part of shaping the platform from day one.
